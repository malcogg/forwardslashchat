## Common Troubleshooting

**AC Not Cooling - Check First:**
1. Thermostat (set to "cool", temp below room temp)
2. Breaker box (circuit not tripped)
3. Air filter (replace if dirty)

**AC Warning Signs:**
- Poor airflow, water leaks, short-cycling, strange odors, odd noises

**Generator Maintenance:**
- Every 6-12 months or after 100 hours runtime

## Key FAQs

**Q: How often should I schedule AC maintenance?**
A: Once yearly in spring. In Florida, twice yearly is better due to heavy usage.

**Q: Can I use Drano for clogs?**
A: No. Drano is corrosive and damages pipes. Professional drain clearing is safer.

**Q: Can I install a generator myself?**
A: Self-installation is strongly discouraged for permanent standby generators. Only portable units should be DIY. Licensed electricians should handle permanent installs for safety and code compliance.

**Q: Should I repair or replace my water heater?**
A: Often worth repairing if relatively new. A professional assessment determines the best course.

**Q: Are your electricians licensed?**
A: Yes, all electricians are licensed and certified.
