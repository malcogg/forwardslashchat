# How to use ForwardSlash.Chat

**Simple guide for customers and teammates**  
Last updated: April 2026

---

## What it is

ForwardSlash.Chat turns **your website** into a **chat assistant** visitors can ask questions. After you sign up and complete checkout, we scan your site, save the text, and the bot answers using that content.

---

## 1. Open the site

Go to your ForwardSlash.Chat URL (for example the production site your team gives you).

---

## 2. (Optional) Scan your website

On the home page you can enter your website address and run a **scan**. That previews how much of your site we can read. It helps you decide if you’re ready to buy.

---

## 3. (Optional) Try the demo chat

Open the **demo chat** page if your team shared a link (often `/chat/demo`). You can try the experience without a full project. You may be asked for contact details; you can **skip** those prompts if you only want to explore.

---

## 4. Sign up or sign in

To order and manage your chatbot, **create an account** or **sign in** (email or Google, depending on how the app is set up).

---

## 5. Pick a plan and pay

Choose the plan that fits your site size and complete **checkout** (secure payment). After payment is confirmed, your order is marked **paid** and you can move on to building the bot.

---

## 6. Build your chatbot (train on your site)

Open your **dashboard**. When your order is paid, use the action to **build** or **crawl** your site. That step reads your pages and saves the content your bot will use. It can take a few minutes for larger sites.

---

## 7. Test your chatbot

In the dashboard you should see a **preview** of the chat. You can also open the **full chat page** link your team provides (it includes your project id). Ask real questions you expect visitors to ask and check the answers.

**On the live chat page**, visitors may see a **short optional contact step** (name / email / phone) they can **skip**. They can also use **shortcuts** like **`/pricing`** or **`/contact`** for quick answers. **Leads** from that flow show in your **dashboard** when visitors choose to share contact info.

---

## 8. Go live on your own domain (if included)

If your plan includes a custom address (for example `chat.yourbusiness.com`), follow the **DNS instructions** in the app or in email: add the record your host asks for, then wait for DNS to update. Your team may attach the domain automatically once DNS is correct.

---

## Need help?

- **Payment or login:** Use the support channel your team shares.  
- **Wrong or missing answers:** Run **build / crawl** again after you change important pages on your site.  
- **DNS or domain:** Double-check the CNAME or record values match exactly what the dashboard shows.

---

*This guide describes the intended customer journey. Exact button labels and URLs may vary slightly by deployment.*
