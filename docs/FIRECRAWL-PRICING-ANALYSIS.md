## Credits Per Customer (Over 2 Years)

- Initial crawl: 50–500 pages (depends on tier).
- Rescans: 7-day cooldown → up to ~52 rescans over 2 years.
- Realistic rescan usage: ~4–8 rescans/year → 8–16 over 2 years.

| Customer tier | Initial | Rescans (×10) | Total credits (2yr) |
|---------------|---------|----------------|----------------------|
| 50 pages      | 50      | 500            | ~550                 |
| 200 pages     | 200     | 2,000          | ~2,200               |
| 500 pages     | 500     | 5,000          | ~5,500               |

Blended (40% / 40% / 20%): ~**2,100 credits per customer over 2 years** ≈ **88 credits/month** per active customer.

## Practical Takeaway

| Scenario                                  | Recommendation                              |
|-------------------------------------------|---------------------------------------------|
| &lt; 30 customers total                   | Hobby ($16/mo) — plenty of headroom        |
| 30–1,000 customers                        | Standard ($83/mo) — comfortable margin     |
| 1,000+ customers                          | Growth ($333/mo) — fits high volume        |

Your pricing clearly covers Firecrawl; the main variable is how often customers rescan. Even with heavy rescans (e.g. 20 over 2 years), Firecrawl stays a small share of revenue.
