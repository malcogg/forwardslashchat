# ForwardSlash.Chat – Investor Overview

**AI Chatbots for Local Businesses. One-Time Payment. No Monthly Fees.**

---

## The Problem

- **Small businesses** (plumbers, clinics, agencies, retail) want to look professional online but lack budget for agencies or complex SaaS.
- **Generic AI chatbots** (Intercom, Drift, Zendesk) cost $50–200+/month, lock businesses into subscriptions, and require technical setup.
- **Website builders** don’t include AI assistants. Businesses that invest in a site still answer the same FAQs manually—phone, email, contact forms.

**Result:** Local businesses miss 24/7 lead capture and support, while visitors leave when they can’t get fast answers.

---

## The Solution

**ForwardSlash.Chat** delivers a **custom AI chatbot** trained on a business’s website. It:

- **Answers visitors 24/7** – Services, hours, prices, FAQs, location
- **Lives on their domain** – `chat.yourbusiness.com` or `yourbusiness.com/chat`
- **One-time payment** – No subscriptions, no monthly fees, no token billing
- **Fast setup** – 3–10 business days from payment to live chatbot

We crawl the site, train the AI, host it, and handle DNS—businesses get a branded chatbot with no ongoing cost.

## Market

- **Target:** Local service businesses (home services, clinics, agencies, retail) in US
- **TAM:** Millions of SMBs with websites; many still using basic contact forms
- **SAM:** Businesses with 10–500 page websites wanting AI without ongoing cost
- **Beachhead:** Florida (Orlando), then Southeast, then national

**Competitive edge:**
- One-time vs. subscription (Intercom, Drift, Zendesk)
- White-label, on their domain vs. widget/embed
- Turnkey: we crawl, train, host vs. self-serve SaaS
